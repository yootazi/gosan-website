01The ThesisREF 001.00

We treat culture not as ornament, but as a macroeconomic driver, an instrument of soft power, and a pillar of national stability. Gōsān designs the institutional, economic and digital blueprints for Iran's transition toward an open future.

Read the prospectus →
02Vision 2600 — The BlueprintREF 002.00

A 15-year blueprint for the revival & prosperity of Iranian arts and culture.

Core directive: to act as an un-censorable digital repository, legal incubator and economic architect for the future of Iranian culture — absorbing all risk from the diaspora while protecting and gathering data from creators on the ground.

Status Active · Offshore 8 strategic goals 15-year roadmap
01
Identity

Cultural Truth, Reconciliation & Identity Revival

Recover, document and restore the authentic national identity and regional heritages the regime suppressed, rewrote or criminalised.

Project · Index of Erased Heritage
02
Free Expression

Institutionalising Freedom of Expression & Anti-Censorship Law

Erect ironclad constitutional and legal barriers so creative expression can never again be weaponised or policed by any authority.

Project · Charter Lab
03
Governance

Independent, "Arms-Length" Cultural Governance

Insulate cultural funding, management and leadership entirely from political regimes — a peer-reviewed, expert-led model.

Project · Governance Library
04
Artists

Elevating the Socio-Economic Status of Creative Workers

Guarantee the financial security, dignity and labour rights of independent artists — turning art from a hazardous gamble into a viable career.

Project · Artist Pulse
05
Economy

Activating the Creative Economy & Infrastructure

Transition culture from a state-subsidised propaganda machine into a profitable, self-sustaining engine that creates jobs and fuels GDP.

Project · Creative Incubator
06
Soft Power

Global Soft Power & Cultural Diplomacy

Rebrand Iran on the world stage — replacing the image of a fundamentalist state with a vibrant 2,600-year civilisation leading a modern renaissance.

Project · Gōsān Gallery
07
Regions

Decentralisation & Regional Cultural Sovereignty

Dismantle the centralised, Tehran-heavy cultural monopoly and empower Iran's provinces to govern, fund and celebrate their local assets.

Project · Regional Portals
08
Digital

Digital Sovereignty, Creative Tech & Cyber-Resilience

Build secure, advanced digital infrastructure to protect the creative economy from state surveillance today and corporate monopolies tomorrow.

Project · Resilient Distribution
