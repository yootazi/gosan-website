Panel 2 — Iran in the world
Culture's place in the economy — Iran vs. the world

Globally, culture is a measured, taxed, fast-growing economic sector. In Iran it is large in people but near-invisible in the national accounts. This panel sets Iran's culture-to-GDP share beside the global average and developing-country peers, and quantifies the missing wealth.
