How cultural institutions are distributed

National counts, and how unevenly they are spread across the 31 provinces once normalised by population. The headline is not the totals — it is the inequality: who is served and who is left out.

Least-served provinces — per 100,000 people
The five provinces with the lowest provision in each category. Red = far below the national average.
The inequality, measured
Gini coefficient of per-capita provision across provinces (0 = perfectly equal, 1 = all in one province).
A library in the best-served province is many times more accessible per person than in the worst. Cinemas are the most unequally distributed cultural good in Iran — and the one most concentrated in a few cities.
