01The Institute

We treat culture not as ornament, but as a macroeconomic driver, an instrument of soft power, and a pillar of national stability. Gōsān designs the institutional, economic and digital blueprints for Iran's transition toward an open future.

Read the Prospectus →
02Mission — Three Pillars
A
De-Radicalization

Institutional Charters

Plug-and-play legal charters that convert state propaganda apparatuses into independent, public-interest platforms — ready on day one.

B
Creative Macroeconomy

A Formal Market

Tax codes, copyright frameworks and co-production compacts that bring the underground and diaspora creative classes into a thriving market that drives GDP.

C
Digital Infrastructure

A Post-Censorship Web

Policy for algorithmic copyright equity, digital-asset protection, and technology to catalogue and secure physical heritage during transition.
